NBC-TV news talks to Dr. Michael Kelly about VECTRA 3D aesthetic simulation
When it comes to breast implants, size really matters. “I wanted to look bigger but I didn't want to be like falling over, but I also didn’t want to be like you couldn’t see a change from what I had before. So I think my biggest issue was getting that right size without overdoing it.” Stephanie Cristobal said. To see what she will look like after surgery, Cristobal had her picture taken with the VECTRA 3D system at Miami Plastic Surgery. Six cameras take simultaneous photos to create 3D images of the patient. The system then creates before and after simulations of breast augmentation surgery.
